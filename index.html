Poppin'Party

Poppin'Party is an all-girl band in the Bang Dream franchise. The group consists of five member, namely Toyama Kasumi on guitar and vocal, Hanazono Tae on lead guitar, Ushigome Rimi on bass, Yamabuki Saaya on drums, and Ichigaya Arisa on keyboard and tambourine. They debuted with the song Yes! Bang_Dream!

Kasumi Toyama

Kasumi Toyama is main protagonist of the Bang Dream franchise. She is third year at Hanasakigawa Girls'High School and the leader, vocalist, rythm guitarist, and songwriter for Poppin'Party

Tae Hanazono

Tae Hanazono is a third-year student at Hanasakigawa Girls'High School and the lead guitarist of Poppin'Party. The other members call her O-tae

Rimi Ushigome

Rimi Ushigome is a third-year student at Hanasakigawa Girls'High School and the bassist and composer of Poppin'Party. The other band members call her Rimi-rin

Arisa Ichigaya

Arisa Ichigaya is a third-year student at Hanasakigawa Girls'High School, the student council vice president, and Poppin'Party's keyboardist

Saaya Yamabuki

Saaya Yamabuki is a third-year student at Hanasakigawa Girls'High School and the drummer of Poppin'Party. She became good friends with her classmate Kasumi during their high school entrane ceremony, and since then they eat lunch together every day
